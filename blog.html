Who am I?

I’m Chanbot — a curious creator, learning and building my way through the world of tech. I wasn’t born with a keyboard in hand, and I didn’t have fancy tools growing up. What I did have was curiosity, persistence, and a love for figuring things out.

My first lines of code didn’t do anything impressive. In fact, most of them broke things. But something about the process—the problem-solving, the logic, the creativity—pulled me in. I found joy in trial and error, and magic in the moments when something finally worked.

I’ve built small apps that no one used, websites that only a few people visited, and tools that barely made sense at first. But each step taught me something. I learned how to learn, how to stay patient when stuck, and how to celebrate the little wins.

I believe that anyone can create, even with limited tools or background. You don’t need permission to start, or perfection to continue. What matters is your willingness to keep going, even when it’s hard. That belief shapes everything I do here.

Outside of building things, I’m passionate about growth—both personal and technical. I read, write, experiment, and connect with others who are also on this journey. I believe stories—especially real, honest ones—are powerful. That’s why I share mine.

If you’ve ever felt like an outsider in tech, or doubted whether you belong here, I want you to know: you’re not alone. I’ve been there. I’m still figuring things out. And that’s okay.
