My Story

Hi there, I’m Chanbot — a tech explorer, self-learner, and creator with a curious mind and a heart full of stories.

My journey began in a quiet little town, far from the buzz of big cities. I didn’t grow up around computers, but the first time I typed a line of code, something sparked. From late-night Googling how to fix errors to experimenting with HTML and building tiny projects no one saw — I was hooked.

This website is more than just a blog. It’s a collection of my personal growth, lessons from failure, and ideas that kept me going. I’ve built apps that broke, made websites that no one visited, and learned more from these moments than from any textbook.

Whether you’re just starting your own digital path, or you’re here for inspiration, I invite you to join me. You’ll find real experiences, honest thoughts, and simple stories from someone who’s still learning every day — just like you.

Thanks for stopping by. The next chapter is just getting started.
